Notable features
(1) A break system.
One image here with picture of tired cartoon
Image of app's break system

You are not a machine, regardless of how much you might aspire to be one.
Com-et offers a simple, yet revolutionary break system that other learning applications do not consider.

Semi-optional breaks are offered throughout each course, allowing you to step outside, check your social media accounts, or enjoy a quick online video.

This is because we know studying can be difficult; headaches, fatigue, and cognitive exhaustion only make it worse. We promise to make it easier on you so that you can reach your learning goals.

(2) Multiple contexts.
image of reviews/pop-quiz

Your memory is better when you repeat things in your head.

Com-et offers a seamless review system that allows for repetition and re-encoding. This means more contexts for you to learn and understand course topics.

(3) Ask a "Tweetcher".
image of twitter

Yes, we came up with that pun.

It can be difficult to get help when you need it, be it due to availability or intimidation - we won't ask.

We integrated Twitter so that you can Tweet your questions directly to a teacher. This means quicker responses and less hesitation.

We are devoted to bringing you closer to your study goals, and hope to see you succeed.
